ABOUT Thrive Innovation Garden Foundation

Innovating for a
Brighter Tomorrow

Thrive Innovation Garden Foundation, based in Akure, Nigeria, is a pioneering NGO dedicated to fostering inclusive and sustainable economic growth. We create economic opportunities that address inequalities and promote job creation by prioritizing innovation and entrepreneurship. Our primary focus is on empowering aspiring entrepreneurs and underrepresented groups, ensuring that economic progress benefits the entire community.

Our Mission

To foster inclusive economic growth by cultivating innovation and entrepreneurship, creating equal opportunities for all, and bridging economic disparities.

Our Vision

To create a world where innovation drives economic empowerment and inclusive prosperity, leaving no one behind in the grand scheme of community impact.

Frequently Asked Questions

Thrive Foundation is an NGO dedicated to promoting innovation and entrepreneurship in emerging communities. We focus on bridging education gaps, fostering digital literacy, and empowering individuals to build sustainable livelihoods.

Our mission is to develop rural and emerging communities by providing access to quality education, entrepreneurship training, and opportunities for economic growth.

Thrive Foundation was founded to address the lack of access to innovation and entrepreneurial support in underserved communities. We aim to create systems that help local businesses and individuals thrive, beginning with the grassroot.

Our programs primarily target students, young entrepreneurs, and individuals in underserved communities. However, we also collaborate with organizations and professionals to expand our reach.
